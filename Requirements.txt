1. Piglatin:
Output of this task should be a working html/javascript application. Write a code that translates a string (word, sentence, or paragraph) into “pig-latin” using the following rules.
a.	Words that start with a consonant have their first letter moved to the end of the word and the letters “ay” added to the end.
i.	Hello becomes Ellohay
b.	Words that start with a vowel have the letters “way” added to the end.
 .	apple becomes appleway
c.	Words that end in “way” are not modified.
 .	stairway stays as stairway
d.	Punctuation must remain in the same relative place from the end of the word.
 .	can’t becomes antca’y
i.	end. becomes endway. (done)
e.	Hyphens are treated as two words
 .	this-thing becomes histay-hingtay
f.	Capitalization must remain in the same place. (done)
 .	Beach becomes Eachbay
i.	McCloud becomes CcLoudmay
